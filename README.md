# Project Description
### Problem

In today’s digital lifestyle, students, programmers, gamers, and office workers spend long hours sitting in front of screens.

This leads to:

* Neck and back pain

* Poor posture

* Eye strain

* Sleep disruption

* Reduced physical activity

Most reminder apps (e.g., drink water or take a break) are ineffective because users simply dismiss notifications without changing their behavior.

There is a lack of engaging and motivating tools that encourage healthy habits consistently.

### Who it helps??
AFK Wellness is designed for:

* University students

* Programmers and developers

* Gamers

* Office workers

* Anyone with a sedentary lifestyle

It helps users develop healthier daily habits in a fun and sustainable way.
